What is Identity Theft?

Identity theft happens when a fraudster acquires your personal information and tries to steal money from your accounts, opens new credit cards, applies for loans and rents apartments, all using your identity.
This can result to damaged credit, unwanted bills and massive time wastage amid frustration while cleaning up the mess.

Monthly Deposits

Individual members contribute a share of not less than Ksh. 1000 to the organization. For groups, each member contributes a monthly share of not less than Ksh.1000 where Ksh. 200 is group share count by the members.
All payments to the organization are monthly and must be made by the last day of the month. Any defaulted payments are penalized at a rate of 10% per month.
All monthly contributions are treated as shares to contributions and attract annual dividends. Dividend rates are up to 60% of the accrued company funds.
